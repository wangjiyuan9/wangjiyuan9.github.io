Research Interests

My research interests focus on leveraging deep learning for 3D geometric perception. I am enthusiastic about exploring CV foundation models or tools such as GAN, Stable Diffusion, FLUX, Qwen-VL, VGGT, etc., and successfully integrating them into my research to achieve impressive results. The specific intersts are listed below:

1) 3D Editing and Perception: Depth Estimation, 3D Gaussian, Normal Estimation

2) Generative Model and Editing Model: Diffusion Model, Flow Matching, DiT. e.g., FLUX-Kontext

3) Others: GRPO (FLow-based), VLM, Contrastive Learning, Curriculum Learning
